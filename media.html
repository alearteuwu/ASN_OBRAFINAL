> THE MAN BEHIND THE COUNTER

Nunca te mete prisa, pero desde el momento en que habla, te sientes tarde.

Se interpone entre tú y el camino; no como guía, sino como recordatorio. Pertenece al lugar.

Tranquilo, contenido, consciente de todos los finales posibles.

Que salgas no le importa, Cuánto tiempo resistes, sí.

Si sigues escuchando, ya has empezado a jugar.

He never rushes you, but the moment he speaks, you feel late.

He stands between you and the way forward; not as a guide, but as a reminder. He belongs to the place.

Calm, composed, already aware of every possible outcome.

Whether you leave is not his concern, How long you last is.

If you're still listening, you've already started playing.
